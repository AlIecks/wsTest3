Our services

anxtream assists clients in managing financial models of any complexity, for any business processes and situations.

We do not trade commodities. Nor do we use templates. Each model we develop is built from scratch with your objectives in mind, and in line with good industry practice.

  • Model building
    • Transactions
    • analytical tools to support negotiation processes and investment decisions
    • Fundraising
    • investor-grade engines for attraction of the necessary financing
    • FP&A
    • rolling forecast and performance management machines
  • Model consulting
    • Testing
    • checking and making sure the model does what it is intended to do
    • Enhancement
    • fixing and augmenting off-hand models
    • Audit
    • second opinion on integrity and reliability of the model

Modeling assignments could be of any nature, from plain calculators to complex simulation engines.

Yet more often than not we help with the following cases of financial models.

    • M&A
  • analytical support for purchase and sale transactions
    • Corporate financing
  • modeling for the purpose of raising financing from international institutions
    • Startup financing
  • modeling for the purpose of raising early-stage financing
    • Project finance
  • modeling of infrastructural projects backed by future cash flows
    • Debt restructuring
  • modeling of future cash flows for determining optimal capital structure
    • Operating models
  • FP&A or business modeling aimed at provision of cash flow projections any moment on demand
    • Valuation
  • modeling for the purpose of determining intrinsic values of assets
    • On-demand analytics
  • analytical support for any business processes and situations
    • Special models
  • modeling of technically complex and non-standard situations using advanced methods
